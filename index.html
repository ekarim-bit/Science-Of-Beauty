Your privacy matters
GenieScan™ uses AI to analyze your hair and generate a personalized profile. Here’s exactly what happens with your data:
  • Photos and quiz answers are sent securely to the AI for analysis only
  • Your photo is not stored, saved, or shared with anyone
  • No account or personal information is required
  • Your hair profile exists only in this session — it disappears when you close the page
